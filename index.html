Single

Pico CMS theme for personal sites

Download the single folder, extract it to the themes folder of your Pico installation, and change the following setting within your config.php: $config['theme'] = 'single';

For the content download our index.md, upload it in the content folder and make your changes.

For the Social Icons we use IcoMoon. You can find a list of all available icons here.

Recommended size for background image: 1920x1384 px

Demo : http://freehtml5.co/demos/single/
